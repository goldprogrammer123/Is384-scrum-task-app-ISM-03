**2. Scrum Events**

Sprint Planning
- The team meets to define the sprint goal.
- Product Backlog items are discussed and selected.
- Tasks are estimated and added to the Sprint Backlog.


 Daily Scrum
- 15-minute daily meeting.
- Each member answers:
  - What did everymember  do for the application development the day before?
  - What will everymember do for the application development today?
  - Any blockers?


 Sprint Review
- Demonstration of completed features.
- Feedback is collected from stakeholders.
- Product Backlog is updated if necessary.


 Sprint Retrospective
- Team reflects on what went well.
- Identify challenges and improvements.
- Action points are documented for the next sprint.


**3. Scrum Artifacts**

Product Backlog
- List of all features and requirements for the app.

 Sprint Backlog
- Tasks selected for the current sprint.

Increment
- A working version of the Task Management App with completed features.
